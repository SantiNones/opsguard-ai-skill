# Onboarding Policy

**Policy ID:** ON  
**Version:** 2024.1  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Owner:** HR Operations + IT  
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## Purpose

Ensure consistent, thorough onboarding experiences that prepare new hires for success while meeting compliance and security requirements.

---

## Rules

### ON-01: Pre-Start Checklist

**Rule:** All new hires must complete pre-start requirements before first day.

**Checklist Items:**
- [ ] Signed offer letter and employment agreement
- [ ] I-9 verification (US) or equivalent (non-US)
- [ ] Direct deposit form submitted
- [ ] Emergency contact information provided
- [ ] Background check cleared
- [ ] Laptop ordered (if remote)
- [ ] Accounts provisioned (email, Slack, systems)

**Deadline:** 3 business days before start date.

**Escalation:** If items incomplete 24 hours before start, notify hiring manager and HRBP.

---

### ON-02: Manager Onboarding Tasks

**Rule:** Hiring managers must complete onboarding preparation tasks.

**Manager Checklist:**
- [ ] Schedule first day meeting (1:1)
- [ ] Identify onboarding buddy
- [ ] Prepare role-specific training plan
- [ ] Set 30/60/90 day goals
- [ ] Schedule team introduction meeting
- [ ] Request system access (if not auto-provisioned)

**Deadline:** 1 business day before employee start date.

---

### ON-03: First Week Requirements

**Rule:** New hires must complete mandatory training in first week.

**Required Training:**
- Security and data privacy (1 hour)
- Code of conduct (30 minutes)
- Benefits enrollment (if applicable)
- Department-specific orientation

**Tracking:** HR monitors completion via LMS.

---

### ON-04: Probation Period

**Rule:** New hires are on probation for first 90 days.

**Probation Implications:**
- Enhanced performance check-ins (weekly for first month)
- Limited vacation accrual (pro-rated)
- Termination procedures simplified during probation

**Completion:** Manager and HRBP sign off at day 90.

---

## Examples

### Successful Onboarding
> New hire submits all pre-start docs 5 days early. Laptop arrives day before start. Manager schedules first week. First day is smooth.

### Delayed Onboarding
> Background check delays completion. Start date pushed by 1 week. Hiring manager notified. Offer extended with new date.

### Incomplete Pre-Start
> New hire hasn't submitted direct deposit form 2 days before start. HR emails reminder. If not resolved by start date, first paycheck delayed to paper check.

### Manager Misses Tasks
> Manager doesn't schedule team intro. New hire feels isolated. HRBP intervenes at week 1 check-in.
