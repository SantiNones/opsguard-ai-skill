# Payroll Adjustments Policy

**Policy ID:** PA  
**Version:** 2024.2  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2024  
**Owner:** Payroll Operations  
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly (due to regulatory changes)

---

## Purpose

Define procedures for payroll account changes, corrections, and adjustments while ensuring timely payment and compliance with banking regulations.

---

## Rules

### PA-01: Payroll Cutoff

**Rule:** Bank account and direct deposit changes must be submitted at least 5 business days before payroll processing.

**Cutoff Schedule:**
- Monthly payroll: Changes due by 5th of month
- Bi-weekly payroll: Changes due by Tuesday 5pm before payday

**Post-Cutoff:** Changes after cutoff require specialist review and may delay payment.

---

### PA-02: Payroll Adjustment Evidence

**Rule:** All payroll adjustments (excluding routine bank changes) require supporting documentation.

**Required Evidence:**
- Time sheet corrections: Manager approval + hours log
- Salary adjustments: HR approval letter
- Bonus payments: Bonus authorization form
- Deduction changes: Employee signed form

---

### PA-03: Payroll Region Impact

**Rule:** Changes affecting tax jurisdiction require review.

**Examples:**
- Moving to different state/country
- Working remotely from new location
- Cross-border work arrangements

**Procedure:**
1. Employee notifies HR of location change
2. Payroll assesses tax implications
3. Legal reviews if cross-border
4. Changes effective next payroll cycle minimum

---

### PA-04: Emergency Changes

**Rule:** Emergency payroll changes (missed deadline, fraud, account closure) require:

1. Written explanation from employee
2. Manager approval
3. Payroll Lead authorization
4. May incur wire transfer fees ($25)

---

## Examples

### Standard Change (On Time)
> Employee submits new bank account info on the 1st. Change effective for 15th payroll.

### Post-Cutoff (High Risk)
> Employee requests change on the 14th for 15th payroll. Requires specialist intervention. Payment may be delayed or sent to old account.

### Cross-Border Impact
> Employee moves from California to Texas. Simple tax update.  
> Employee works from Portugal for 2 weeks. Requires Legal review per PA-03.
